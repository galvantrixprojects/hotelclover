LOCATIONS

It’s all about the location. Your stay at Hotel Clover Asoke will place you in the heart of Sukhumvit, Bangkok’s longest street. The main street is a dynamic hive of activity throughout the year, with upscale shopping malls, popular restaurants and trendy bars. The magic though, happens on its adjoining ‘sois’ (alleys), where you will find everything from hipster cafes, to bustling markets, and colourful nightlife.
