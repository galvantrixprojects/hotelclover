ABOUT HCG

Hotel Clover is a collection of modern, innovative boutique hotels in Asia which offer:

Unique themes. Each of our concept-driven hotels tells a distinct story, no two are alike. Bespoke designs and carefully curated décor create an immersive and memorable stay for our guests.

World-class hospitality. We are passionate about service and hospitality, and constantly seek new ways to pamper our guests. Modern in-room amenities and hotel facilities ensure your stay with us is comfortable and welcoming.

Exceptional locations. Whichever city we are in, Hotel Clover is looking to be at the heart of it all. We are located in interesting neighbourhoods, to provide a fresh and authentic experience of each city.

DISCOVER OUR PROPERTIES

Singapore

HOTEL CLOVER 33 JALAN SULTAN

An urban sanctuary, nestled in the heritage district of Kampong Glam. Hotel Clover 33 Jalan Sultan transforms a row of conserved two-storey shophouses into a tranquil respite from the hustle and bustle of modern day Singapore. Come back to lush greenery and rich wooden interiors, featuring handpicked furniture and art pieces from around South-East Asia.

Singapore

HOTEL CLOVER 769 NORTH BRIDGE ROAD

Step into 1950s Singapore. Hotel Clover 769 retains the original exterior of its conserved two-storey shophouses that were constructed between 1840 and 1960, and has filled its interior with vintage decorative pieces. Enjoy the unique blend of old-world charm and modern day luxury that this charming boutique hotel has to offer.

Singapore

HOTEL CLOVER 5 HONGKONG STREET

A home away from home for business and leisure travellers. The modern-style rooms are well-furnished with practical amenities and little luxuries to ensure you are comfortable during your stay with us. You’ll enjoy being located in Clarke Quay, a historical landmark which is now home to an electric mix of trendy restaurants, alfresco riverside dining and lively bars.

Bangkok, Thailand

HOTEL CLOVER ASOKE

Indulge in life’s little luxuries during your stay at Hotel Clover Asoke. Unwind in one of our specially designed rooms, have a swim in our unique glass-bottom pool, and take in spectacular views of the city while enjoying a drink at our rooftop bar. Located on Sukhumvit, Bangkok’s longest street, Hotel Clover Asoke puts you right in the heart of the city. The perfect abode for experiencing the best of what modern Bangkok has to offer.
